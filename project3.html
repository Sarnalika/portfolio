Pizza Sales Analysis

Objective

This project demonstrates how **data analysis techniques** can be used to provide a pizza restaurant with actionable insights into their **sales performance**. The goal is to help the restaurant understand **customer preferences** and optimize business decisions.

Problem Statement

The restaurant struggled to identify its **best-selling pizzas**, peak sales hours, and customer preferences. Without **data-driven insights**, making informed decisions about pricing, promotions, and inventory management was difficult.

Dataset

The dataset includes **sales transactions** from the pizza restaurant, containing information such as:

  • Order ID and Date
  • Pizza Type and Size
  • Quantity Sold
  • Total Sales Amount
  • Customer Location
The data was extracted using **SQL** for further analysis.

Approach

  • Extracted and cleaned sales data using **SQL**.
  • Created **interactive dashboards** in **Power BI** to visualize sales trends.
  • Analyzed **best-selling pizzas**, revenue trends, and customer purchasing behavior.
  • Identified peak sales hours and seasonal trends to optimize pricing and promotions.

Outcome

The Power BI dashboard provided key insights, helping the restaurant:

  • Identify the **most popular pizza types** based on sales volume.
  • Optimize inventory management to reduce food waste.
  • Adjust marketing strategies based on peak sales times.
  • Increase revenue by focusing on high-performing pizza categories.
